Frequently Asked Questions

What is the duration of the internship?

The internship duration is flexible and depends on the track you choose. Typically, it ranges from 2 to 4 weeks.

Do I need prior experience to apply?

No prior experience is required. We welcome beginners and provide guidance throughout the internship.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes, you will receive a certificate upon successful completion of the internship.

Do you provide an offer letter?

Yes, we provide an offer letter to all selected candidates at the beginning of the internship.

Are there any rewards for top performers?

Yes, we recognize and honor top performers by showcasing their achievements on our LinkedIn page. This is a great way to gain visibility and build your professional network!

How do I apply?

Click the "Apply Now" button above and fill out the application form. We'll get back to you shortly!
